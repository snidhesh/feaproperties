The story

Why a boutique, anyway?

I spent the first half of my career inside large brokerages — the kind where lists are long and follow-ups are templated. I learned the market there. I also learned, sharply, what I didn't want to build.

FEA Properties exists for clients who'd rather have one phone number than ten. Who want a single advisor across rentals, off-plan investments, and resale exits. Who appreciate that boutique doesn't mean small — it means deliberate.

We're licensed, registered, and regulated under Dubai's RERA. The numbers we run will match yours; the contracts we draft will hold up. And the conversation, more often than not, will be the part you remember.

— Feleg
