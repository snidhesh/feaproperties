Overview

An address that needs no introduction.

Como Residences rises on the western crescent of Palm Jumeirah — a slender, sculptural tower of seventy-six storeys, designed to disappear into the sea on one side and the city on the other. Every residence occupies a full floor; the duplexes occupy two. The penthouse occupies the sky.

Interiors are by a Milanese atelier; the lobby art is commissioned; the private beach is gated; the home automation is invisible. Whether you're moving in, investing, or simply collecting addresses — this is one of the few in Dubai that is both an investment and an art piece.
